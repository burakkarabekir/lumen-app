DESCRIPTION (2695/4000)
Let your day belong somewhere.

Some days there is no one to tell. Some days the words scatter the moment you try. Lumen is for exactly that: a calm, private space to leave what is on your mind, as it is. Write long, write short, or write a single line — it all counts.

It is different from a notes app: when you want, you get a reflection that looks at what you wrote with a second pair of eyes.

Everything below is in the free version.

Write and keep
• Create an entry with a title and text; pick the date and time yourself.
• Mark how you feel from 17 moods — Happy, Grateful, Calm, Focused, Nostalgic, Tired, Stressed, Melancholic and more.
• Add a topic, search your entries, filter by type, and favourite the ones you come back to.

Bring the moment with you
• Add a photo from your gallery or camera to any entry (up to 25 MB).
• Add your location and a link — small details that bring a day back years later.

AI reflection
• One free reflection a week: a personal look at what you wrote, and a question to sit with.
• Every reflection comes with its own AI-generated cover art — in the free version too.
• Your quota renews every Monday.

Insights
• See your streak, total entries and word count.
• Track how often you write with a monthly chart.
This screen is free and identical for Plus users.

Your journal is yours
• App lock with Face ID.
• Cloud sync; your entries are kept on servers hosted in Europe and synced across your devices.
• Export your journal to PDF, share an entry as an image.
• Auto / Light / Dark theme, 5 languages, and delete your account whenever you want.

What Lumen Plus adds
Plus adds exactly three things on top of the free version:
1) Up to 7 AI reflections a day — instead of 1 a week on the free plan.
2) A look back over your last 7 days on the Insights screen: a personal write-up of the themes standing out in your entries from the past seven days. Not tied to a set day; it refreshes as you write.
3) Video and voice recording — one of each per entry, up to 25 MB each.

Plus does not unlock these, because they are already free: photo and camera attachments, the reflection cover art, and all the stats on the Insights screen.

The yearly plan includes a 7-day free trial. Prices appear in the app, in your region's currency. Your subscription renews automatically at the end of the period; you can manage or cancel it anytime.

A few notes
• AI reflections can make mistakes; they are private to you.
• Lumen is not a mental-health service; it does not offer diagnosis or treatment.
• If you are going through a hard time, please reach out to someone you trust or a professional.

How did today go? Lumen is here when you are ready to write.
